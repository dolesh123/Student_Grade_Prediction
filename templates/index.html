🎓 Student Grade Predictor

Predict any student's score based on multiple factors

Any subject name
Total hours in the entire week (e.g., 5 hrs/day × 5 days = 25 hrs)
Class attendance percentage
Previous exam/test marks
Average hours of sleep per night
Average hours spent on social media daily
Self-rated stress level (0 = no stress, 100 = maximum stress)

📊 Feature Impact Guide:

study_hours (weekly) → Positive 📈 (More study = Higher score)

attendance → Positive 📈 (Higher attendance = Higher score)

previous_marks → Strong Positive 🔥 (Strongest impact!)

sleep → Balanced ⚖️ (7-8 hours is optimal)

social_media → Negative 📉 (Less time = Higher score)

stress → Mixed ⚖️ (Some stress helps, too much hurts)
